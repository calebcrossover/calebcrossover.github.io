Caleb Crossover is an account which stars and is ran by Kaleb (stylized "Caleb"). It is also effectively his stage name.

Kaleb originally came up with the name while he was shooting hoops. Kaleb suddenly though of a street name, and a specific name, "Caleb Crossover," just seemed to stick.

Eventually, Kaleb fell out of love with basketball. The name was ultimately repurposed from a street name to his official stage name!

Hence, CALEB CROSSOVER was born.
